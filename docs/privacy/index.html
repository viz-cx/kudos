Privacy Policy

Your gratitude, your control.

Last updated: 10 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Kudos app (“Kudos,” “we,” “us”) handles information when you use it. We designed Kudos to collect as little as possible.

Information we handle

  • Account credentials. To send kudos you sign in with a VIZ account. Your username and private key are stored only on your device, in the iOS Keychain. We do not transmit or store your private key on any server we control.
  • Kudos you send and receive. Kudos are recorded on the public VIZ blockchain. This means the sender, recipient, appreciation level, and message are public and permanent by the nature of the blockchain, and are not something we can edit or delete on your behalf.
  • Camera. With your permission, the camera is used solely to scan a recipient's QR code. Images are processed on-device in real time and are never stored or uploaded.

What we do not do

  • We do not include third-party advertising or analytics/tracking SDKs.
  • We do not sell or rent your personal information.
  • We do not collect your contacts, location, or browsing activity.

How information is used

Information is used only to provide the app's core function: signing you in, letting you send kudos to the VIZ blockchain, and displaying your personal history of received and sent kudos. Communication with VIZ blockchain nodes happens over standard encrypted connections.

Third parties

Kudos communicates with public VIZ blockchain nodes to read and write kudos. These nodes are operated independently of us. Data written to the blockchain is publicly readable by anyone, consistent with how public blockchains work.

Data retention & deletion

Credentials stored in the Keychain are removed from your device when you sign out or delete the app. Kudos already recorded on the VIZ blockchain cannot be deleted, as blockchain records are immutable. To request help with your account, contact us at the email below.

Children's privacy

Kudos is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
