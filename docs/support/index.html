Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account to receive kudos?

No. Anyone can receive kudos. You only need a free Kudos account to send them.

How do I send kudos to someone?

Open the Kudos tab, search for a recipient (or scan their personal QR code), choose an appreciation level, add a short note, and tap “Send kudos.”

Where can I see the kudos I've received or sent?

Open the You tab. You'll find your personal history of received and sent kudos — tap any entry for the full detail.

Why does Kudos ask for camera access?

The camera is used only to scan a friend's QR code when adding a recipient. It is never required for the main flow and no images are stored.

How do I delete my account or data?

Email us at babin@axveer.com and we'll help you remove your account and associated data.
